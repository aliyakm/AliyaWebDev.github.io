Recipe #1: Potato Chips

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Unpeeled medium potatoes 7
Ice water 2 quarts
Salt 5 teaspoons
Garlic powder 2 teaspoons
Oil for deep-fat frying Amount depends on you

Step 1: Using a vegetable peeler or metal cheese slicer, cut potatoes into very thin slices. Place in a large bowl; add ice water and salt. Soak for 30 minutes.

Step 2:Drain potatoes; place on paper towels and pat dry. In a small bowl, combine the garlic powder, celery salt and pepper; set aside.

Step 3:In a cast-iron or other heavy skillet, heat 1-1/2 in. oil to 375°. Fry potatoes in batches until golden brown, 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently.

Step 4:Remove with a slotted spoon; drain on paper towels. Immediately sprinkle with seasoning mixture. Store in an airtight container.

Recipe #2: Garlic-Cheese Crescent Rolls

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
refrigerated crescent rolls 8 ounces
butter, melted 3 tablespoons
garlic powder 1-1/2 teaspoons
dried oregano 1 teaspoon
grated Parmesan cheese 2 tablespoons

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375°. Separate crescent dough into eight triangles. Roll up from the wide end and place point side down 2 in. apart on an ungreased baking sheet. Curve ends to form a crescent.

Step 2:Combine butter, garlic powder and oregano; brush over rolls. Sprinkle with cheese.

Step 3:Bake until golden brown, 10-12 minutes. Serve warm.

Recipe #3: Herbed Potato Dumplings

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
egg, lightly beaten 1
seasoned dry bread crumbs 3/4 cup
all-purpose flour 1 tablespoon
minced fresh parsley 1 tablespoon
minced chives 1 tablespoon
minced fresh thyme 1-1/2 teaspoons
salt 1/2 teaspoon
pepper 1/2 teaspoon
finely shredded uncooked potatoes 2-1/2 cups

Step 1:In a large bowl, mix the first eight ingredients. Stir in potatoes. Shape into 1-1/2-in. balls; dust with flour.

Step 2:Drop dumplings on top of simmering soup or stew of your choice. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook 30 minutes or until dumplings are cooked through. Serve immediately.
